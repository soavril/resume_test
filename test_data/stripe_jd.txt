Stripe - Senior Backend Engineer, Payments Platform

Location: Remote (US, Canada, or APAC)
Team: Payments Infrastructure

About the role:
We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Payments Platform team.
You'll work on the core systems that process hundreds of billions of dollars annually
for millions of businesses worldwide.

What you'll do:
- Design, build, and maintain critical payment processing infrastructure
- Work on distributed systems that handle millions of transactions per second
- Improve system reliability, latency, and throughput of our payment APIs
- Collaborate with product teams to build new payment features and methods
- Drive technical decisions and contribute to our engineering culture
- Mentor engineers and help grow the team's capabilities

We're looking for someone who has:
- 5+ years of experience in backend software engineering
- Strong proficiency in at least one of: Java, Kotlin, Go, or Ruby
- Experience building and operating large-scale distributed systems
- Deep understanding of database systems (relational and/or NoSQL)
- Experience with event-driven architectures and message queuing systems
- Track record of designing APIs consumed by external developers
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail

Nice to have:
- Experience in the payments or fintech industry
- Familiarity with Kubernetes and containerized deployments
- Experience with observability tools (Datadog, Grafana, etc.)
- Contributions to open source projects
- Experience working in globally distributed teams

What we offer:
- Competitive salary ($180K - $280K) + equity
- Remote-first culture
- Learning & development budget
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) with company match
